    Getting Started

    • Attention: At this time, I am unavailable for large scale development projects (> 10hrs/week) until September 2nd, 2019. Availability for design and small scale development projects is the same.
    • Process
      1. Get in touch with me!

        Send an email to lex@lexusdrumgold.design with your first and last name, project name, budget, ideal timeframe, and a project description. The project description should include:

        1. A 5 to 7 sentence overview of your project.
        2. An outline of the primary features you'd like to see in the first release of your project.
        3. An outline of the secondary features, or nice to haves.

        All the above information is required before moving onto the Consultation phase.
        Please allow up to 48 hours for a response.

      2. Consultation - $50

        This can be done remotely (via Google Hangouts) or in person at my local Starbucks, and will be scheduled via email. During this time we will review the details of your project - deliverables, deadlines, and budget. Should we decide to work together, the following will happen:

        1. Contact information will be updated to include your name, address, phone number, and email.
        2. A project proposal, which includes a deliverables timeline and project price, will be drafted. The consultation fee will then be credited to your account and later deducted from the total project fee.
        Cancellations and appointment time changes must be made at least two hours in advance or you will not receive a refund. If you are more than 15 minutes late for your consultation, your appointment will be cancelled without a refund.
      3. Finalized Proposal & Contract

        Before starting work, the project proposal needs to be signed, as well as a contract.

      4. Project Begins!

        After signing the proposal and contract, 50% of the project fee will be due. The remaining fee will be spread across the remaining weeks of the deliverable timeline. With each completed deliverable, an invoice will be sent and must be completed before the start of the next feature, otherwise the project will be delayed. Late payments will incur a 10% fee.

      5. Deliverables
        At the start of the project, we will work together to get you set up with Github so that you can review my work, request and/or approve changes, as well keep up with the project timeline and weekly updates. At the end of the project, you will receive instructions detailing how to interact with your newly completed project. If maintenance is requested, a new contract will be opened, which can be priced at an hourly or retainer rate.
