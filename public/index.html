    • I offer UI/UX design and development for web, mobile, and desktop environments. Below you'll find an overview of my pricing model and how to start a project with me. Project pricing is dependent upon project scope, timeframe, and my talent as well :)
    • Process
      1. Get in touch with me!
        Send an email to lex@lexusdrumgold.design with your name, your budget, the name of your project, your ideal timeframe for the project, and a thorough project description. Please allow up to 24 hours for a response. I check my email Monday through Friday, from 9am to 5pm.

      2. Consultation - $50
        This can be done remotely (via Slack video call) or in person (depending on location). During this time we will go over the details of your project and decide if I'm a good fit. Should we decide to work together, a detailed project plan, including pricing, timeframe, and deliverables will be developed. The consultation fee will then be credited to your account and later deducted from the total project fee.

        Schedule an appointment: Remote | Onsite

      3. ### **Finalized Proposal & Contract + Contact Information Update**
        Before starting work, a design and/or development proposal needs to be signed, as well as a contract. Contact information will also be updated to include your name, address, phone number, and email.

      4. Project Begins!
        After signing the proposal and contract, 50% of the project fee will be due three days before the project start date.
        The remaining fee will due at project completion.
